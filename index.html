About me

Swordsman

I'm a master swordsman and one of the strongest members of the Straw Hat Pirates, led by Monkey D. Luffy. My dream is to become the world's greatest swordsman by defeating Dracule Mihawk, the current titleholder. I specialize in a unique three-sword style (Santoryu), wielding one sword in each hand and the third in my mouth. My dedication to my training and my unwavering loyalty to my friends define who I am. Although I have a poor sense of direction, I make up for it with my combat skills and determination. I'm always ready to fight for my crew and our dreams!
